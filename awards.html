Awards

  • ORDERS

    • The Order of the Quarter Horse

      • A Quarter Horse is allowed to wear a brown colored tabbard with the household's symbol on it.
      • One token will be given to each founding member of the Cavalry of Sword Horse.
      • The member can give this token to anyone they feel worthy of being a 'Quarter Horse'
      • If a Quarter Horse comes to the encampment bearing the coin, they get a free drink from the Cavalry. (once per event)
      • If a member of the Cavalry goes to the quarter horse's encampment and they cannot produce the token, the quarter horse must provide the Cavalry member an alcoholic beverage. (once per event)
